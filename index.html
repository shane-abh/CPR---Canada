About Us

Absolutely love Canada? Eager to witness God shower blessings on our beautiful nation? Excited about being a part of a prayer movement that has the potential to make a global impact? If your response is a resounding "yes" to any of these questions, we warmly invite you to join us at the Canada Prayer Rally (CPR).

We are not just an event; we're a nationwide prayer movement that brings together Christians from all walks of life, backgrounds, and denominations, uniting in a common purpose—to pray for Canada. Picture this: prayer rallies happening in cities across the country where we come together to lift our voices in unity, praying for our local communities, leaders, our nation, and the world. It's more than just an event; it's a time of worship, connection, and heartfelt requests to God for His guidance and blessings upon our land.

Why do we believe in prayer so strongly? Because we see it as the mightiest tool in our Christian arsenal. Prayer has the incredible power to move mountains, change hearts, and transform entire nations. We're not just asking for your presence; we're inviting you to be a part of a movement that calls on Christians to take action. Let's harness the power of prayer together, making a positive impact not only in our communities but also in our beloved nation. Your involvement matters, and your prayers can be a force for good! Come, be a part of something bigger—join us at the Canada Prayer Rally.
